Welcome the “Our 20th Century” Red Carpet Chronicles. This is where you can view mini-documentaries on every motion picture nominated for an Academy Award™ in every major category, including best actor, best actress, best director, and best film for each year from 1929 to 1980.

Each mini-doc will share the back stories and history of each film, together with anecdotes, facts, jealousies, drama and triumphs that played out behind-the-scenes on the quest for the coveted gold statuette. Each week new mini-docs will be featured that chronicle the history of filmmaking, and share with viewers the genesis of the films and the people who played a major role in the making of these films.
